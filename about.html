Therian-ness

Though I am human in physical form, that just isn't me. My ideal form would not be human, but rather something with fur, dexterous paws, digitigrade legs, and a prehensile tail; beyond that, though, it's pretty all over the place. I've made a graph to try and get that variation concisely summed up (see to the right), but in the future I may try my paw at making something I can just throw percentages in to get a graph out of.

While the blobs of colour represent traits, the outlined boxes represent forms. Overlap means hybridization. Basically, if you threw a dart and it landed within at least one of the boxes, it'd be roughly the same probability as that form feeling ideal at the moment.

One of the forms listed (Amphimorpho) is a species by TuxedoDragon. They have a page that explains them far better.
